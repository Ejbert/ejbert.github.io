RestoList is a way to track all of the restaurants that you've visited, and all those you still want to try. Build your list based on the latest restaurant reviews, friend recommendations, preferred cuisine, or type of occasion. Next time you're put on the spot to find a great meal, pull up your pre-organized list.

After you've visited, leave notes on your favorite dishes, best tables or time to arrive. Or, think it makes a great place for when the 'rents come to town? Add it to a new list!
